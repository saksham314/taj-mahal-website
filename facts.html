The Taj Mahal: A Timeless Wonder

A Journey Through History and Beauty

Unveiling the Taj Mahal's Fascination

  • A Love Story in Marble: Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, it's a lasting testament to love and loss. Learn more about the love story.
  • A Marvel of Mughal Architecture: Combining Persian, Indian, and Islamic styles, the Taj Mahal's white marble facade features intricate floral patterns and calligraphy.
  • 22 Years in the Making: Construction began in 1632, employing over 20,000 artisans who mastered diverse skills in masonry, stone carving, and inlay work.
  • The Power of Symmetry: The Taj Mahal is known for its perfect symmetry across its north-south axis, reflecting a harmonious balance in design.
  • A Dazzling Reflection: The reflecting pool in front creates a mesmerizing mirror image, enhancing the monument's grandeur. Did you know it's not actually a pool, but a man-made canal?
